Key Features :

1. Interactive videos and visuals: Teaches about disasters in an engaging manner.
2. Scenarios based quizzes: Test what you know about disaster response
3. Facts vs Myths: Clear misconceptions and build trust in safety procedures
4. Real life stories of survivors: Helps people understand how others faced tough situations, learn from their experiences and feel encouraged and supported during difficult times .
5. Zoomable maps: Helps people easily explore disaster-prone areas, see where risks are higher and find nearby emergency shelters and hospitals when needed.
