FLOODS

What is a Flood?

A flood occurs when water overflows from rivers, lakes, or seas onto normally dry land.
It can result from heavy rainfall, dam failure, melting glaciers, or cyclones, and can cause property damage, water contamination, injuries, and even fatalities.
